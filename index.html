Meet Lady Lotus
🌸

Hi, I'm Lady Lotus

Lady of the Womb

I built The Lotus Assembly because too many women are navigating their bodies, their health, and their womanhood without the information they were always owed. As the Lady of the Womb, I try my hardest to lead with intentional education, integrity, and a deep belief that every woman has the right to know herself fully — whether it be physically, emotionally, spiritually, or politically.

My mission is to close the gap between what women are told and what they actually deserve to know — about their bodies, their rights, and the issues that directly affect their lives. This is not a space for surface-level conversation. This is where women go deeper and where women come to rise.

If my page resonated with you, join a cause or come find us in the Discord. There is a seat here for you.
